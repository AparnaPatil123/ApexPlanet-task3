# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Objective
This dashboard analyzes sales performance using Power BI. It provides key business insights through KPIs, charts, and interactive filters.

## KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Quantity
- Average Order Value

## Visualizations
- Sales by Product
- Sales by Category
- Sales by City
- Monthly Sales Trend

## Filters
- Category
- City

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop

## Insights
- Electronics generated the highest sales.
- Mobile and Laptop products contributed the most revenue.
- Sales performance can be filtered by city and category.
